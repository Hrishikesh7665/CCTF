About The CTF

CDAC Capture The Flag is a cybersecurity competition designed and organized by Information Security Services, CDAC Kolkata in collaboration with DISA [Department of Information Security Assurance] where participants compete by solving challenges that involve finding and exploiting vulnerabilities in simulated environment.

  • Date: 09th of August, 2024
  • Who can participate: CDAC Employees and Students
